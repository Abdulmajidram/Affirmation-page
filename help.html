Focus and Stress Relief Tips for Students

Focus Tips

  • Create a designated workspace free from distractions
  • Break large tasks into smaller, manageable ones
  • Set specific goals and deadlines for each task
  • Eliminate or limit sources of distraction, such as social media or phone notifications
  • Take regular breaks to rest your mind and prevent burnout

Stress Relief Tips

  • Exercise regularly to release endorphins and reduce stress
  • Practice deep breathing, meditation, or other relaxation techniques
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet
  • Get enough sleep to recharge your body and mind
  • Connect with others and talk about your feelings and concerns
