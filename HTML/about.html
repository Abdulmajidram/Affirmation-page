About Us

This page was created by Gayle, Andriana and Abdulmajid as a group project as part of our course at Code Your Future (CYF)

Our Aim

Our aim for this project was to tackle feeling associated with learning and just life in general. We wanted to create affirmations for students originally and people in general to either improve or enhance their mood based on their feelings in the moment. Based on whatever mood you are feeling, there will be quotes to uplift you and encourage you to be positive and improve your mood.
