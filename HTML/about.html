About Us

This affirmation site is a buddy-group project, created by students: Abdulmajid, Andriana and Gayle, as part of our course module at Code Your Future (CYF).

Our Aim

Our aim with this project was to tackle feelings associated with studying, as well as life in general. We wanted to create affirmations for students to help them improve or enhance their mood, based on how they are feeling in a particular moment. Whatever your mood, we have tried to provide some quotes that we hope will uplift you and encourage you to be positive and resillient.
